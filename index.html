Intro

Hello World, I am Loxelheim!

Or Loxel for short! My real name is Alex(ander) though, if you want to be all proper and formal. But I'll be honest, I don't think much of honorifics, so, feel free to be casual, yeh?

I'm a learned Media Designer and I worked as a Dev for a handful of years. A wild mix, since in my experience people tend to be either-or and only rarely both.

In short: I like artsy-tartsy-shiny-sparkly stuff, as well as the cold, hard, logic of Code.

It's a difficult balance to keep, but I enjoy it both!

And here is the default recommended slide structure I've used as a basis for my upcoming exam presentation. Not MY recommendation, but by Syntax Institute (shoutout to Mete! 👋🏻)

Copypasted with love 💖 Formatted and kept In German, of course. Dated: March 7th.

Folie Titel Zeit Inhalt
1 Titel + Zielgruppe 0:30 Thema, dein Name, Zielgruppe nennen
2 Ausgangslage 1:00 Problem, Impact, wer war betroffen
3 Ziel & Scope 1:00 Was wollte ich erreichen? Abgrenzung
4 Vorgehen (Teil 1) 1:30 Analyse, erste Schritte, Hypothesen
5 Vorgehen (Teil 2) 1:30 Durchführung, Entscheidungen
6 Ergebnis 1:30 Was wurde erreicht? Nachweis, Nutzen
7 Lessons Learned 1:30 Was würde ich anders machen? Risiken
8 Zusammenfassung 1:00 Kernaussagen, Ausblick, Dank

I think it's a solid structure for presentations, when you have to show something to a group of people with varying depths of knowledge.

And it's a fantastic guide for creating your own. Which helped me a bunch, that's for sure!
